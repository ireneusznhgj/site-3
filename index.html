Dent Studio Connect App

To streamline patient care and B2B laboratory orders, DENT STUDIO SP Z O O has launched a proprietary mobile HealthTech platform.

The Dent Studio Connect app bridges the gap between our clinics, the dental laboratory, and our patients. Users can schedule appointments, securely view 3D oral scans, and track the fabrication of dental prosthetics directly from their Android devices.

  • Smart Booking: Instantly schedule visits with our health practitioners.
  • Digital Medical Records: Secure access to personalized treatment plans.
  • 3D Scan Viewer: Review your dental imaging and aligner progression.
  • B2B Lab Portal: Partner clinics can track prosthetic fabrication statuses.
